About

I am a 5th-year CS PhD candidate in EECS department of University of Michigan. My advisor is Professor Morley Mao. I received my B.Eng. in Communication Engineering from Beijing University of Posts and Telecommunications(BUPT). I am also fortunate to have spent a year and a half at Tsinghua University working with Professor Jun Bi (RIP) on SDN.

I am generally interested in networking and computer systems. My research focuses on designing and implementing novel architectures, abstractions and interfaces for Software-Defined Networks(SDN).
